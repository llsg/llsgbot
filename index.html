Setting Guide

If you are group leader then you may want to use setting command, it requires level 5 from you. !setting get/set/delete [setting] [value]
Possible settings:
- INACTIVITY_DAYS [number]
- COOLDOWN [True/False]
- ADD_CHECK [True/False]
- WELCOME_MESSAGE [message and {name} for senderhandle]
- COOKIE_SYSTEM [True/False]
- COOKIE_TIME [number (default: 60)]
